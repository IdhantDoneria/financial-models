# FINMODELS — Advertisement Campaign & 10-Day Outreach Plan (PMS & boutique hedge funds)

Audience: founders/PMs of small PMS firms and hedge funds (2–15 people) running
continuous equity + derivatives modelling for serious investors. Every creative shares
the terminal's visual identity — near-black background, amber monospace command line,
signed green/red data colors — so the ad *looks like the product* and the click delivers
exactly what the ad promised (message-scent consistency).

---

## The three campaign creatives

### Campaign 1 — "The Anchor" (LinkedIn feed / Instagram)
- **Hook:** `BLOOMBERG SEAT: $25,000/YR` · `JUNIOR ANALYST: $80,000/YR` (both struck through) → `YOUR WHOLE FUND: {{price}}`
- **Body:** Ten institutional models — Heston to DCF — live in the browser. Validated to machine precision. No install, no IT, no data contract.
- **CTA:** Run your book through it free → {{site_url}}
- **Psychology:** double price anchoring (seat + salary), contrast effect, low-friction trial.

### Campaign 2 — "The Unstressed Number" (poster / story / retargeting)
- **Hook:** `THE MOST EXPENSIVE NUMBER AT A FUND IS THE ONE NOBODY STRESSED.`
- **Body:** Bear/base/bull scenarios, tornado charts, two-way sensitivity grids — every
  cell a real model run. See which assumption your valuation lives or dies on, before
  your investors ask.
- **CTA:** Stress-test the book → {{site_url}}
- **Psychology:** loss aversion aimed at the fund manager's core fear — being wrong in
  front of an allocator; curiosity gap ("which assumption?").

### Campaign 3 — "Filing In. Report Out." (conversion / email header)
- **Hook:** `FILING IN. INVESTOR-READY REPORT OUT. 2 MINUTES.`
- **Body:** Drop in any company's annual or quarterly report → financials auto-extracted →
  IB-grade assumptions with live sovereign rates (15 markets) → ten models → PDF/Excel
  report for the client meeting.
- **CTA:** Founding-fund pricing — {{price}}, locked for life, first {{N}} funds.
- **Psychology:** concreteness (the workflow is the ad), effort-saving vividness, honest
  capacity scarcity + lifetime-lock endowment.

## 10-day outreach calendar

Rules of engagement: **every day touches 10–20 new prospects** (email/DM) *plus* one
public-platform asset that compounds. Rotate the marketing *type* daily so no channel
fatigues and each prospect meets the product from a different angle. Track everything in
a simple sheet: name · fund · channel · day · response · next step.

**Prep before Day 1 (one afternoon):**
- Build a 150-name prospect list (LinkedIn Sales-style search: "founder/CIO/PM,
  portfolio management OR hedge fund, 2–50 employees"; PMS registries; fund databases;
  fintwit follows). 15 names/day × 10 days.
- Set up a booking link (Calendly-style), a tracking sheet, and the three Canva creatives.

| Day | Theme | Platform(s) | Marketing type | The move |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | The time-bleed audit | Cold email | Direct outreach (pain-led) | Email 1 to 15 funds. Public: LinkedIn post announcing the terminal with Campaign 1 creative. |
| **2** | Show, don't tell | LinkedIn DM + X/Twitter | Social selling + content | DM 15 founders with the personalized opener. Post a 60–90s screen recording: `HES <GO>`, drag vol-of-vol, smile repricing live. Raw terminal footage outperforms polish. |
| **3** | Reciprocity strike | Email + WhatsApp (warm) | Free-work marketing | Pick 10 prospects, run one of *their* holdings through the desk, send the actual PDF report (Email 2). Ask warm contacts for 2 intros each. |
| **4** | Authority content | LinkedIn article + Reddit | Thought leadership | Publish "Why every valuation should ship with a tornado chart" — teach the method, terminal appears only in screenshots. Share to r/quant, r/SecurityAnalysis as genuine discussion (no link-drop; value first, product in profile). |
| **5** | The credibility angle | Cold email (batch 2) + Instagram | Direct outreach + paid-style creative | Email 3 (investor-audit frame) to 15 new funds. Post Campaign 2 creative ("the unstressed number") as IG/LinkedIn image ad — boost with small spend if available. |
| **6** | Live demo event | LinkedIn Event + email invite | Webinar marketing | Announce a 25-minute open demo, "Stress-testing a live book in real time," 4 days out. Invite every prospect touched Days 1–5. Scarcity: cap attendance ("first 25 seats") — honestly. |
| **7** | Personal video | Loom via email/DM | 1:1 video outreach | For the 10 hottest prospects: 2-minute personal Loom running *their* sector through SCEN, their domicile in the market selector. "Made this for you" — unignorable reciprocity + effort signal. |
| **8** | Derivatives desk day | X/Twitter thread + Discord/Slack quant communities | Technical content | Thread: "Heston vs Black-Scholes on the same book — what stochastic vol changes, in 6 charts" (all from the terminal, benchmarks cited). Engineers and quants at funds are your Trojan-horse champions. |
| **9** | The webinar | Live (LinkedIn/Zoom) | Event + assumptive close | Run the Day-6 demo. Let an attendee name a stock and model it live. End with founding-fund offer + booking link. Same-day recap email with recording to all no-shows (second bite). |
| **10** | Close week | Email + phone/DM | Follow-up + takeaway close | Email 4 (breakup + founding-fund deadline) to every non-responder. Personal follow-up to every demo attendee with the two-option close (license vs. two-week pilot). Book next-week calls; the pipeline, not Day 10, is the finish line. |

**After Day 10:** repeat the cycle on the next 150 names with the messaging that won —
judge by *demos booked per channel*, not likes. Ask every pilot fund for one
referenceable sentence; from cycle 2 onward, real peer proof replaces every borrowed
authority claim in this document.

## Measurement
- One variable per test (subject line, then hook, then creative).
- North-star: guest session → demo booked → pilot started. Vanity metrics ignored.
- Expected honest funnel at this list quality: ~30–40% email opens, 5–10% replies,
  2–5 demos/cycle, 1–2 pilots. Plan capacity (and scarcity claims) around that.
