The three metrics

Every model is measured by an automated scorer — scores are computed, not asserted.

1 · Pedagogical clarity

Google-style docstrings, formulas tied to inline comments, LaTeX derivations, worked examples, and an interactive chart per model.

2 · Numerical accuracy

Validated against Hull's option tables, closed-form identities, and live Fama-French data — with machine-precision benchmarks.

3 · Production readiness

Full input validation, structured logging, type hints, custom exceptions, and an extensible base-class architecture.
